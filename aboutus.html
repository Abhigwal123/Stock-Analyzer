About

Welcome to our Stock Analyzer web app!

We are dedicated to providing you with powerful tools to analyze and predict stock market trends.

Stock Analysis

Our stock analysis tool utilizes advanced algorithms and machine learning models to provide accurate predictions and insights into stock market movements.

Technology Used

Our Stock Analyzer is built using Flask, a lightweight WSGI web application framework, and TensorFlow, a powerful open-source machine learning library, to predict stock market movements.
